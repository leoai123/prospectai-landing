Questions You Might Have

Is the data collection legal?
Yes. The system only collects publicly available business contact information (company websites, business directories, public LinkedIn pages), fully compliant with GDPR and B2B commercial email regulations.
Will it get my company email blacklisted?
No. The system includes email warming, sending frequency controls, and email verification — a triple-layer protection that keeps bounce rates under 2% and actively maintains your domain reputation.
Can AI personalize for niche industries?
Absolutely. Just describe your product characteristics and target audience. The AI learns industry terminology and buyer pain points — whether it's industrial parts, agricultural machinery, or software services.
Do I need technical or AI knowledge?
Not at all. The interface is designed for sales professionals — it's as simple as filling out a form. We also provide free 1-on-1 onboarding to get you running on day one.
Can I review emails before they're sent?
Yes. The system offers both "fully automatic" and "manual review" modes. You can have the system generate drafts for your team to review before sending — balancing efficiency with quality control.
How quickly will I see results?
Typically, the first batch of emails goes out within 24 hours of setup. Most clients start receiving replies by week 2-3. The system continuously optimizes copy, so month 2 typically outperforms month 1.
